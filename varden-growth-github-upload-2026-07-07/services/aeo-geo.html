AI visibility

Make your business easier for AI systems to understand and include.

AI visibility is not magic. It is influenced by clear service information, strong business entities, reviews, trusted third-party evidence, reputation, authority, and useful content.

AI platforms need more than a few optimized pages.

AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and other answer platforms rely on public information. If your business is inconsistent, thin, or hard to verify, AI systems have less confidence. If the business is clear across the website, profiles, reviews, directories, and authority sources, it becomes easier to summarize and recommend.

Clear answers

Service pages and FAQs that answer buyer questions about services, fit, timing, process, risk, pricing context, and next steps.

Entity consistency

Public business facts that agree across Google, Apple, Bing, directories, profiles, schema, and third-party references.

Trusted evidence

Reviews, photos, testimonials, local mentions, industry profiles, and authority signals that support trust beyond the website.

Answer readiness

SEO helps the site rank. Visibility operations help the business get understood.

AEO and GEO are useful terms, but they are not separate magic services. They are part of a broader system that makes the business clearer across the public web.

What gets improved

The goal is to make the business easier to understand, not to promise guaranteed placement inside any AI platform.

  • Business fact audit across website, profiles, listings, and public sources
  • Service pages written around real buyer questions and decision criteria
  • FAQ and answer hubs for high-intent local topics
  • Schema for business, services, breadcrumbs, and FAQs where appropriate
  • Review, proof, local authority, and third-party evidence planning
  • Observed AI mentions and citations where they can be checked
