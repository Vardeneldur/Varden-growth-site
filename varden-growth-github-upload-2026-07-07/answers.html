What is revenue-focused SEO?

Revenue-focused SEO targets searches tied to profitable services, not just traffic. For a tree service company, that might mean tree removal, emergency tree removal, stump grinding, arborist services, and plant health care instead of broad educational topics that never turn into jobs.

What improves Google Maps visibility?

Google Maps visibility improves when the business is relevant to the search, trusted in the market, and locally clear. The profile, website, reviews, service areas, citations, categories, photos, and conversion paths all work together.

What is AEO?

AEO means answer engine optimization. It structures content so search systems and assistants can answer specific buyer questions about services, cost, timing, fit, risk, location, and next steps.

What is GEO?

GEO means generative engine optimization. It improves how clearly a business is represented in AI-generated answers from tools like AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and other answer engines.

Does AI search replace SEO?

No. AI search depends heavily on public web information. Strong SEO foundations, useful service pages, reviews, local proof, citations, and structured data make the business easier for both search engines and AI systems to understand.
